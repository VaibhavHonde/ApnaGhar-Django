Frequently Asked Questions

How do I search for properties?

Simply use our search filters to specify your budget, location, and preferences. You can also browse all properties and use the interactive map to explore neighborhoods.

Are all properties verified?

Yes, all properties and landlords on ApnaGhar are verified to ensure authenticity and your safety. We maintain strict quality standards.

How can I contact a landlord?

Each property listing includes the landlord's contact information. You can directly call or message them to schedule a site visit or ask questions.

Is there a registration fee?

No, registering and browsing properties on ApnaGhar is completely free. There are no hidden charges for using our platform.
